CHILI

(1 protein, 1 vegetable)

* 4 oz. ground sirloin
* 1 tomato
* 1/8 tsp. minced onion or onion powder
* 1/4 cup water
* Chili powder to taste
* Garlic powder to taste

1. Brown meat and drain.
2. Chop tomato or puree in blender.
3. Combine all ingredients and simmer.
